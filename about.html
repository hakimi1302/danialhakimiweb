ABOUT ME

HELLO

I am Danial Hakimi. I've always wanted to introduce myself as "D", so that I wouldn't have to tell people my real name, I would have preferred to use an alias instead of my own name, but I guess that isn't possible in real life. I'm sorry if the picture of the calligraphic written letter "D" does not satisfy my dear visitors on my website, as I really do not like to show my face to people. I've always been like that, but most of the time I'm forced to do it as it is a requirement in some places like some online classes or presentations.

In my whole 20 years of living, for the past 13 years, I've spent most of my time in front of my computer. I don't know why but I just find the online world fascinating. I think by the fact that I browsed and read some realities about this world on the internet had matured me at such a young age. I did well in primary school. But things changed in high school as I purposely flunked my exam results. All that solely for a reason that I didn't want to be recognized by people. As life goes on, I got into a college, despite having such bad grades in SPM. In reality, I'm just lucky that I have special privileges just because I'm Malay, because I know, I don't deserve to get into a college with bad results. I say that because I have friends who are not Malay, got 8A's and above but still, they are struggling to get into any college.

Everything changed when I got into a college. I've used almost my full potential in intelligence on most subjects and got 3 Dean Lists in a row. Although I didn't take some of the subjects seriously, but for some reason I managed to do that. But really, do i deserve to have this kind of knowledge? Some of my classmates, I know, were trying their best but they are nowhere near to my pointers. While I'm just playing around, I didn't even study for most of the exams, while they are pulling all-nighters for it. I don't understand why God gave me this ability. Really, I don't think I deserve those pointers at all. More or less, there is nothing interesting about me, that's all I can say.
